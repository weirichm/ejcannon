Elyse Cannon

Artist & Photographer

I'm Elyse Cannon. My love for photography started after I had my first child and the desire to capture every moment in my life has grown with each little one I bring in to the world. I started out only working with family and friends and the was the second shooter for a local wedding photographer. I have recently branched out on my own and look forward to helping you capture the big events in your life. Natural light and bright colors are found throughout my photos. I am patient with children and able to work the long, energy filled hours of a wedding day. I am located in East Charlotte, NC and I am able to travel around the Carolinas. Being able to photograph babies, birthday parties, weddings and family portraits is what brings joy into my life. I would be honored to be chosen to photograph your special event.
